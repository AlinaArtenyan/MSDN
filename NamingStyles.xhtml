If you choose a naming style, it is good to be consistent!
XML document often have a corresponding database. A good practice is to use the naming rules of your database for the elements in the XML documents.

There are 5 predefined entity references in XML:

&lt; < less than
&gt; > greater than
&amp; & ampersand
&apos; ' apostrophe
&quot; " qoutation mark

Note: Only the characters "<" and "&" are strictly ilegal in XML. The greater than character is legal, but it is a good habit to replace it.
